Kimbap

Description

Kimbap is well-balanced korean dish which is also very easy to eat.

Kimbap is very popular food to bring to a school if there is sports day.

Ingredients

  • 4 dried seaweed sheets
  • 120g spinach
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1/2 carrot
  • 4 imitation crab sticks
  • 4 sticks of ham
  • 4 yellow radish pickle sticks

Steps

  1. Cook rice in bowl or rice cooker
  2. While rice is cooking, boil spinach and add some salt after its cooked.
  3. Cut carrot into small pieces and stir fry in pan with ham.
  4. Once its all cooked, take out the carrot and ham from the pan.
  5. Add some oil to the pan and put egg into the pan.
  6. Cut cooked egg into straight lines.
  7. Add sesame oil into rice once its done.
  8. Paste rice into seaweed.
  9. Add ingredients on top of the rice.
  10. Roll the kimbap with enough force to keep everything together.
